Five Governance Practices. One Honest Assessment.

Focus Mindset

Does your board operate with a clear, outcomes-focused governing philosophy?

Clarify Priorities

Has your board set specific, measurable student outcome goals?

Monitor Progress

Does your board systematically track whether students are achieving those goals?

Align Resources

Does your board allocate budget, staff, and time based on student outcome priorities?

Communicate Results

Does your board transparently share outcome progress with the community?

Each practice is scored and benchmarked against boards nationally — so you can see not just how you score, but how you compare.
